Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I learn offline?

A: Yes! All HTML and markdown files can be downloaded and used offline. Just download the files and open them locally in your browser.

Q: Do I need ServiceNow access?

A: Recommended for exercises. Get a free developer instance at developer.servicenow.com. The course can be studied without access, but hands-on practice requires it.

Q: Can I share this with my team?

A: Yes! The course is MIT licensed and fully shareable. Perfect for team training and organizational use.

Q: Will this help me get a job?

A: Absolutely! This course leads to ServiceNow CPQ certifications, builds a portfolio of real projects, and teaches highly hireable skills.

Q: Is this beginner-friendly?

A: Yes! The course starts from complete basics with no prior CPQ experience required. All levels from beginner to advanced are covered.

Q: What if I get stuck?

A: Check the course materials first (usually has answers), review the glossary for terminology, look at case studies for similar examples, study the exercises for working examples, or consult ServiceNow official documentation.
